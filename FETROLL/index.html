About the creator
Mary, cloud specialist

Hey there! I'm Mary and I with my little studio lives in Wroclaw, Poland.

I have tried and experimented quite a lot to create a mobile, that would be 100% unique. Thus I invented my first clouds and since then my house has become a real cloud workspace.

For 3 years I have worked as an inventor, designer, photographer, director, manager, copywriter and even a postal courier at Fetroll studio. It’s me who answers all your questions, who collects your mobiles and sends parcels all over the world.

I am very proud to make adorable decor for your nursery and to help you create one and only interior for your sweet little ones. Thank you so much for choosig Fetroll studio!

Why choose Fetroll
Here are 3 main reasons why you may choose Fetroll cloud mobiles, garlands and wall hanging decor.
1. Originality
Clouds are created using a unique technology. They are soft, textured, keep their shape. You will not find such realistic clouds in any baby’s toy workshops.
2. Experience
Over 3 years of work in the studio, more than 2,600 clouds have been created, mobiles live in 36 countries of the world, already 264 babys admire the clouds over their cribs.
3. Handmade
Creating clouds and decorations, assembling mobiles, packing and shipping orders - everything is done by hand with attention to every detail.
